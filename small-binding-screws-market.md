<p><strong>The Small Binding Screws Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slotted (Minus Hole)</li><li>Cross-recess (Plus Hole)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The small binding screws market consists of various types, primarily slotted and cross-recess screws. Slotted screws feature a single horizontal groove on the head, designed for flat-blade screwdrivers, making them straightforward yet less efficient for drive engagement. In contrast, cross-recess screws, also known as Phillips screws, have a cross-shaped recess that allows for better torque transfer and reduced slipping, accommodating a wider array of screwdrivers. Both types are essential in various applications, from electronics to furniture assembly.</p></p>
