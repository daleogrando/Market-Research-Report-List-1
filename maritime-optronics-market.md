<p><p>The Maritime Optronics market encompasses various technologies, including multispectral and hyperspectral imaging. Multispectral imaging captures data across several specific wavelengths, allowing for enhanced target detection and classification in maritime environments. In contrast, hyperspectral imaging involves capturing a broader range of wavelengths, providing detailed spectral information for precise analysis of materials and objects. Both technologies facilitate improved surveillance, reconnaissance, and environmental monitoring in maritime operations, aiding in defense, search and rescue, and resource management applications.</p></p>
