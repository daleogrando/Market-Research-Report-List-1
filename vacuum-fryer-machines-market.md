<p><p>Vacuum fryer machines come in two primary types: batch and continuous. Batch type vacuum fryers process a limited volume of food in discrete cycles, allowing for flexibility in production and minimal setup changes. They are ideal for small businesses or specialty products. In contrast, continuous vacuum fryers operate in a continuous flow, providing consistent output and efficiency for large-scale production. This type is favored by high-volume manufacturers seeking to streamline operations while maintaining product quality.</p></p>
