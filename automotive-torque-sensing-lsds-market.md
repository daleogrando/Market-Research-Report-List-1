<p><p>The automotive torque sensing limited-slip differentials (LSDs) market is primarily categorized into three types: cone-type LSDs, plate-type LSDs, and others. Cone-type LSDs utilize conical surfaces to engage locking mechanisms, providing effective torque transfer. Plate-type LSDs feature multiple friction plates that create slip resistance, enhancing traction. Other types may include designs like viscous LSDs or electronically controlled systems. Each type offers distinct advantages in performance, handling, and application suitability, catering to various automotive requirements and driver preferences.</p></p>
