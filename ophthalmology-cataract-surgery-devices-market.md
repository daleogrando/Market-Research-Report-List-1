<p><p>The ophthalmology cataract surgery devices market includes several key product types. Intraocular lenses (IOLs) are artificial lenses implanted to replace the eye's natural lens after cataract removal. Phacoemulsification devices utilize ultrasound technology to break up and suction out cataracts, facilitating efficient surgery. Ophthalmic viscoelastic devices (OVDs) are used to maintain eye structure and protect tissues during surgery, providing a fluid environment that enhances visibility and space for the surgeon. Together, these devices play a crucial role in successful cataract procedures.</p></p>
