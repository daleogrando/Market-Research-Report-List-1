<p><p>The recycling carbon fiber market encompasses various processes to repurpose carbon fiber materials, primarily focusing on chopped and milled recycling. Chopped recycling involves cutting carbon fiber waste into smaller fibers, which can be reused in composites and other applications, ensuring materials are utilized effectively. Milled recycling, on the other hand, transforms carbon fiber into a fine powder, enabling its incorporation into different products, enhancing strength and durability while reducing waste. Both methods contribute to sustainability in the composites industry.</p></p>
