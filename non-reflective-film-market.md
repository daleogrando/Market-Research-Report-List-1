<p><p>The non-reflective film market is categorized into three main types: TAC-based films, PET-based films, and others. TAC (Triacetate Cellulose) films are known for their excellent optical clarity and durability, primarily used in displays and optical applications. PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) films offer good thermal stability and mechanical strength, making them suitable for various industrial uses. The "others" category encompasses alternative materials, such as polycarbonate and special coatings, which are utilized for specific applications requiring unique properties.</p></p>
