<p><p>The food freeze-drying equipment market is primarily divided into two types: batch freeze-drying equipment and continuous freeze-drying equipment. Batch freeze-drying equipment processes small, discrete quantities of food in cycles, making it ideal for smaller producers or specialty items. In contrast, continuous freeze-drying equipment is designed for high-volume production, continuously feeding and processing food, thus offering greater efficiency and scalability. Both types cater to different production needs, influencing operational costs and product quality across the food processing sector.</p></p>
