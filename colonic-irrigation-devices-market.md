<p><strong>The Colonic Irrigation Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Open System Machine</li><li>Close System Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The colonic irrigation devices market is primarily categorized into open system machines and closed system machines. Open system machines utilize gravity to facilitate the flow of water during the irrigation process, allowing the user more control over the experience. In contrast, closed system machines are designed with a sealed pathway that minimizes the risk of contamination, offering a more regulated and hygienic approach. Both types cater to different preferences and requirements in therapeutic and wellness settings.</p></p>
