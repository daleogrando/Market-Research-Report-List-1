<p><p>The Oral Root Canal Sealer Market encompasses various types, each offering unique properties for endodontic treatment. Dioxide Mineral Aggregate sealants promote healing and tissue compatibility. Sealants containing bioceramics are known for their bioactivity and sealing efficiency. Encapsulants with glass ionomer provide fluoride release, and calcium hydroxide sealants enhance antibacterial effects, promoting root canal success.</p></p>
