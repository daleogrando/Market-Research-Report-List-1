<p><p>The Optical Thickness Measurement System Market is categorized into contact and non-contact types. Contact optical thickness measurement involves direct physical interaction with the material, allowing precise readings but potentially affecting the sample's integrity. Non-contact measurement utilizes laser or optical technologies to gauge thickness from a distance, ensuring sample preservation and enabling measurements of delicate or moving surfaces. Both methods serve diverse industries, including manufacturing, textiles, and electronics, providing critical data for quality control and process optimization.</p></p>
