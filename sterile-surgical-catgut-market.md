<p><p>The sterile surgical catgut market encompasses two primary types: plain catgut and chromic catgut. Plain catgut, derived from the intestines of sheep or goats, is a natural absorbable suture used for soft tissue approximation. Chromic catgut is treated with chromic salts to prolong its absorption time and reduce tissue reactivity, making it suitable for more prolonged healing processes. Both types are essential in surgical procedures where sutures need to dissolve naturally over time, minimizing the need for removal.</p></p>
