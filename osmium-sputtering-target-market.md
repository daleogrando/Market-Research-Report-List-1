<p><p>The osmium sputtering target market features two main types: rotary and non-rotatable. Rotary type targets allow for uniform erosion across the surface during the sputtering process, enhancing material utilization and reducing waste. This type is ideal for large-area coatings. In contrast, non-rotatable targets are fixed in position and typically used for smaller applications or specific geometries. Both types serve key roles in various industries, including electronics and optics, depending on the required coating characteristics and efficiency.</p></p>
