<p><p>The Automated Fluid Dispensing Systems market includes three primary types: valves, pumps, and dispensers. Valves control the flow and direction of fluids, ensuring precise output and preventing leaks. Pumps are responsible for moving fluids, offering various types such as diaphragm, gear, and peristaltic, each suited for specific applications. Dispensers accurately measure and release fixed amounts of fluid, enhancing efficiency in processes like manufacturing and laboratory work. Together, these components facilitate automation, improve accuracy, and increase productivity across diverse industries.</p></p>
