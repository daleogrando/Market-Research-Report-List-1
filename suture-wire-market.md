<p><strong>The Suture Wire Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Suture Wire</li><li>Non-Absorbable Suture Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The suture wire market is primarily divided into two types: absorbable and non-absorbable suture wires. Absorbable suture wires are designed to dissolve in the body over time, eliminating the need for removal, and are commonly used in internal procedures where long-term support is not necessary. In contrast, non-absorbable suture wires remain intact and are used in situations requiring permanent support, such as orthopedic and cardiovascular surgeries. Both types cater to specific surgical needs, influencing their market demand and application areas.</p></p>
