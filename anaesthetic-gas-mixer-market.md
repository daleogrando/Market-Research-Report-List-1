<p><p>The anaesthetic gas mixer market comprises two main types: high-pressure blenders and filling blenders. High-pressure blenders are designed to mix gases at elevated pressures, ensuring precise delivery of anaesthetic agents for surgical procedures. They typically feature advanced controls for accurate gas mixture ratios. In contrast, filling blenders are used to fill gas cylinders with specific anaesthetic gas mixtures, focusing on efficient and safe filling processes. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing patient safety and operational efficiency in healthcare settings.</p></p>
