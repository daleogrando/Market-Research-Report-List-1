<p><strong>The Stone Gypsum Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Natural Type</li><li>FGD Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Stone Gypsum market comprises two primary types: Natural Gypsum and Flue Gas Desulfurization (FGD) Gypsum. Natural Gypsum is mined from geological deposits and is prized for its purity and quality, making it ideal for applications in construction and agriculture. FGD Gypsum, on the other hand, is produced as a by-product during the process of removing sulfur from flue gases in power plants. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as cement production, plasterboard manufacturing, and soil conditioning.</p></p>
