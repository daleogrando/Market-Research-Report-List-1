<p><p>The Portable Interactive Whiteboard market is primarily divided into two types: Front Projection and Rear Projection. Front Projection systems utilize an overhead projector that casts images onto the whiteboard surface from the front, allowing viewers to interact with the display. In contrast, Rear Projection setups project images from behind the board, enabling a clearer view without obstructions. Each type offers unique advantages, including installation flexibility and space efficiency, catering to diverse educational and corporate environments.</p></p>
