<p><p>Pharmaceutical Contract Research and Manufacturing (CRAM) encompasses services provided by Contract Research Organizations (CROs) and Contract Manufacturing Organizations (CMOs). CROs offer research services, including clinical trials and data management, aiding pharmaceutical companies in the drug development process. In contrast, CMOs focus on the production of drugs, handling both active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs) and finished products. Together, CROs and CMOs streamline the drug development and manufacturing process, allowing pharmaceutical companies to reduce costs and enhance efficiency without compromising quality.</p></p>
