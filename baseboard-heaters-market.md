<p><p>The baseboard heaters market consists of two primary types: electric and hydronic. Electric baseboard heaters are powered by electricity and offer quick heating, making them ideal for individual room temperature control. They are easy to install and maintain. In contrast, hydronic baseboard heaters use hot water circulating through pipes to generate warmth, providing a more consistent and energy-efficient heat. Both types cater to different consumer needs, preferences, and installation scenarios in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
