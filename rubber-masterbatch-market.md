<p><strong>The Rubber Masterbatch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>EPDM-based Type</li><li>SBR-based Type</li><li>NBR-based Type</li><li>Natural Rubber-based Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rubber masterbatch market comprises several types based on the polymer used. EPDM-based masterbatches offer excellent weather resistance and ozone stability, making them suitable for automotive and construction applications. SBR-based types are favored for their good abrasion resistance and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in tires and industrial products. NBR-based masterbatches are known for oil resistance, ideal for automotive and industrial seals. Natural rubber-based types provide high elasticity and strength, frequently used in consumer goods and adhesives, enhancing overall performance in various applications.</p></p>
