<p><p>Melamine-faced panels for flooring, specifically the MF-C and MF-MDF types, are engineered wood products designed to enhance durability and aesthetic appeal. MF-C panels feature a core material, often particleboard, coated with melamine resin to provide water resistance and scratch resistance. MF-MDF panels, on the other hand, utilize medium-density fiberboard as the base, offering a smoother surface for intricate designs. Both types are popular in residential and commercial flooring applications due to their versatility, cost-effectiveness, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
