The market is primarily segmented into two types: fluorometric assays and colorimetric assays. Fluorometric assays utilize fluorescence to detect B-hydroxybutyrate with high sensitivity, making them suitable for low concentration samples. In contrast, colorimetric assays employ color change reactions, offering simplicity and ease of use for routine analysis. Both types cater to varying laboratory needs and preferences, driving market growth.</p></p>
