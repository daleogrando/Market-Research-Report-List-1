<p><strong>The Fiber Optic Temperature Measurement System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluorescence Fiber Temperature Measurement</li><li>Distributed Optical Fiber Temperature Measurement</li><li>Fiber Bragg Grating Thermometry</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Optic Temperature Measurement System market encompasses several types: </p><p>1. **Fluorescence Fiber Temperature Measurement** utilizes luminescent materials to detect temperature changes via light emission variations.  </p><p>2. **Distributed Optical Fiber Temperature Measurement** employs scattered light in the fiber to provide continuous temperature readings along its length.  </p><p>3. **Fiber Bragg Grating Thermometry** uses periodic refractive index changes in the fiber to detect temperature shifts, changing the reflected light wavelength.  </p><p>4. **Others** include various niche methods and technologies not categorized above, catering to specific applications and industries.</p></p>
