<p><p>The stainless steel alloy market is segmented into three main types: austenitic, ferritic, and martensitic. Austenitic stainless steels, known for their excellent corrosion resistance and formability, dominate the market. Ferritic alloys offer good resistance to stress corrosion and are cost-effective, primarily used in automotive applications. Martensitic stainless steels, characterized by high strength and hardness, are typically used in tools and cutlery. Each type serves distinct applications, driving diverse demand across industries from construction to manufacturing.</p></p>
