<p><p>The line voltage smart thermostats market is classified primarily into two types based on wiring configurations: single pole and double pole wiring. Single pole wiring typically controls heaters with a single power supply, making it suitable for simpler heating systems. In contrast, double pole wiring is designed for more complex setups, allowing for control of higher voltage appliances and providing enhanced safety. These distinctions cater to varying home heating requirements, bolstering energy efficiency and user convenience in temperature management.</p></p>
