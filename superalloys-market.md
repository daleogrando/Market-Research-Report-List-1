<p><p>The superalloys market is primarily segmented into three types: nickel-based, nickel-iron based, and cobalt-based superalloys. Nickel-based superalloys are renowned for their high-temperature strength and oxidation resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and gas turbine applications. Nickel-iron based superalloys combine good mechanical properties with thermal resistance, often used in automotive and industrial sectors. Cobalt-based superalloys excel in high-temperature stability and corrosion resistance, utilized in both aerospace and medical implants, providing tailored solutions across various industries.</p></p>
