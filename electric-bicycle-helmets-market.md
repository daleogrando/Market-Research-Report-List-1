<p><p>The electric bicycle helmets market encompasses three main types: full face helmets, open face helmets, and half helmets. Full face helmets offer maximum protection, covering the entire head and face, making them ideal for high-speed or off-road riding. Open face helmets provide coverage for the top and back of the head while allowing for a greater field of vision and airflow, suitable for urban commuting. Half helmets are lightweight and offer minimal coverage, appealing to riders seeking comfort and style during short trips.</p></p>
