<p><p>The multilayer ceramic substrate market comprises primarily two types: aluminum nitride (AlN) and alumina (Al2O3) multilayered ceramic substrates. Aluminum nitride substrates are known for their high thermal conductivity, making them suitable for power electronics and high-frequency applications. In contrast, alumina substrates are valued for their excellent insulating properties and cost-effectiveness, widely used in various electronic devices. Both types cater to specific industry needs, including automotive, telecommunications, and consumer electronics, enhancing performance and reliability in electronic circuits.</p></p>
