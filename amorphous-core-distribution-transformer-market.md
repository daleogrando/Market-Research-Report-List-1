<p><p>The amorphous core distribution transformer market comprises two primary types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed transformers use insulating oil to cool and protect the core and windings, offering efficient heat dissipation and enhanced performance in various environments. In contrast, dry-type transformers utilize air for cooling, making them safer for indoor applications and areas with environmental concerns. Both types benefit from amorphous core technology, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing losses, thus meeting the growing demand for sustainable electrical solutions.</p></p>
