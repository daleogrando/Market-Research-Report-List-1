<p><p>The organic tomato powders market is primarily categorized into two types: spray-dried and freeze-dried. Spray-dried tomato powder is produced by rapidly drying tomato puree with hot air, resulting in a fine, shelf-stable product ideal for culinary applications. In contrast, freeze-dried tomato powder is made by freezing the tomatoes and then removing moisture under vacuum, preserving more nutrients and flavor, making it suitable for high-quality food products and rehydration in gourmet cooking. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs and preferences.</p></p>
