<p><ul><li>Contact-based EMV Cards</li><li>Contactless EMV Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The EMV cards market includes two primary types: contact-based and contactless EMV cards. Contact-based EMV cards require physical insertion into a card reader, ensuring secure transactions through chip technology. This type is widely used in traditional retail environments. Conversely, contactless EMV cards use near-field communication (NFC) technology, allowing payments by simply tapping the card on a compatible terminal, enhancing convenience and speed. Both types are aimed at improving security and reducing fraud in electronic transactions.</p></p>
