<p><p>The magnesium carbonate market is primarily divided into two types: light magnesium carbonate and heavy magnesium carbonate. Light magnesium carbonate is characterized by its finer particles and is used in applications such as pharmaceuticals, food additives, and personal care products due to its high purity and absorbent properties. In contrast, heavy magnesium carbonate, with larger particle sizes, is commonly utilized in industrial applications, including construction materials and fireproofing, due to its greater structural stability and thermal resistance.</p></p>
