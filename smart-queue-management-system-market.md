<p><p>The Smart Queue Management System market primarily consists of two types: Virtual Queue and Linear Queue. A Virtual Queue allows customers to join a queue remotely via apps or SMS, enabling them to wait comfortably until their turn is called, enhancing the customer experience. In contrast, the Linear Queue operates on a first-come, first-served basis, where customers physically line up in sequence. Both systems aim to improve customer flow, reduce wait times, and optimize service efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
