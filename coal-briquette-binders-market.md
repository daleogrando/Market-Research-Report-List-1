<p><p>The coal briquette binders market is categorized into three main types: organic, inorganic, and compound binders. Organic binders, derived from natural materials like starch and lignin, provide excellent adhesion and are eco-friendly. Inorganic binders, such as cement and clay, offer strong binding properties and are often more cost-effective. Compound binders combine both organic and inorganic materials, resulting in enhanced performance, durability, and environmental benefits. Each type serves specific applications based on desired briquette characteristics and production processes.</p></p>
