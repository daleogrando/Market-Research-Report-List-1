<p><strong>The DC Servo Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Brush Motor</li><li>Brushless Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The DC servo motors market comprises two main types: brushed and brushless motors. Brushed DC servo motors utilize brushes and a commutator to deliver torque, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness but requiring maintenance due to wear on brushes. In contrast, brushless DC servo motors eliminate brushes, employing electronic controllers for efficient performance, higher reliability, and reduced maintenance. While brushed motors are often favored for low-cost applications, brushless motors are increasingly popular in precision tasks requiring durability and superior control.</p></p>
