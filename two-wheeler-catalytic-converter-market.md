<p><p>The Two-Wheeler Catalytic Converter market features several technologies for reducing emissions. Selective Catalytic Reduction (SCR) utilizes a reductant to convert nitrogen oxides (NOx) into harmless nitrogen and water. Lean NOx Catalysts (LNC) facilitate NOx reduction in lean-burn conditions, while Lean NOx Traps (LNT) capture and convert NOx during specific conditions. Other market types may include different catalytic technologies or combinations aimed at enhancing efficiency and compliance with stringent emission regulations for two-wheelers.</p></p>
