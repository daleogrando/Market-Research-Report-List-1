<p><p>Rocket packs can utilize different propellant types, primarily categorized into gases and liquids. Gas-based rockets use compressed gases, providing immediate thrust but generally offering lower efficiency and limited burn times. Liquid rocket packs, on the other hand, store propellants in liquid form, allowing for greater control over thrust and extended burn durations. Liquid systems can often achieve higher performance and are suitable for more complex missions, while gas systems are simpler and more portable, catering to various applications.</p></p>
