<p><p>The flooring foams market comprises three primary types: polyethylene, ethylene vinyl acetate (EVA), and polyurethane. Polyethylene foam is lightweight and moisture-resistant, making it ideal for underlays and cushioning. EVA foam is known for its flexibility and shock absorption, often used in athletic flooring and mats. Polyurethane foam offers superior durability and comfort, frequently found in high-end flooring solutions. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying performance requirements in residential and commercial flooring.</p></p>
